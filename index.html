Things to consider during the task

  • How do you feel when trying to book the flight? Frustrated, completely fine, it was easy to do
  • What improvements do you think the website you were on could make to improve the experience you are having?
  • Do you think the people making the website considered other languages and cultures when initially making the website? Or were they an after thought?

Chayn Brief

  • Abuse of any sort is normalised in many cultures
  • When conducting user research be aware of different cultures and tailor your questions for different cultures and other intersections of our society (e.g. different religious groups)
  • How Western society sees abuse is not the same in various communities across London, even if you do not agree it's important to be respectful of other's views so you can tailor your questions accordingly
